Our Simple Process

From your first inquiry to final handover — straightforward and stress-free.

1

Inquiry & Consultation

Tell us what you need. We'll advise on the best materials and approach for your project.

2

Site Visit & Measurement

Our team visits your site, takes accurate measurements, and assesses the installation conditions.

3

Quotation & Approval

We provide a detailed, itemised quote. Once approved, we confirm the schedule and begin fabrication.

4

Fabrication & Delivery

All items are precision-fabricated in our workshop, then delivered and installed by our own team.

5

Inspection & Handover

Final walkthrough with the client. We don't close the job until you're 100% satisfied.
